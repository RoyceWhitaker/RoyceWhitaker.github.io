Frankenstein


Location: England

Time Period: 1820's

Frankenstein’s monster is a human-like creature that was created by putting together different body parts which were collected from various graveyards and butcher shops. Victor Frankenstein used chemistry and electricity to bring the creature to life. The monster is over eight feet tall with yellow skin and has stitches and marks all over its body. During the time Frankenstein’s Monster was created, Europe was in an age of unprecedented scientific discovery. Biology was at the forefront of intellectual inquiry at that time. Anatomist found new ways to reanimate the dead and many experiments were conducted to give life to non-living things. At the beginning of the 19th century, electricity was recently discovered and scientists at that time had the assumption that electricity and magnetism could bring the dead back to life. Scientist were able cause movements in non-living things by passing electricity through their corpses. Of course, the movements were just electricity passing through the nerves. However, for the people in that time period, they saw these experiments as life being manipulated by the hands of the scientists, acting as God.
