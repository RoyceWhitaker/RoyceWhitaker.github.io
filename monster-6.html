Babadook


Location: North America

Time Period: 1st seen 1700's

The Bogeyman is a monster that has been around since the 1700’s. Depending on the culture, there are are several different ways to spell Bogeyman such as Boogeyman, Bogieman, and Boogie Man. The Bogeyman is said to be a mythical creature who has taken part in a multitude of cultures. He is often told in folklore that he strictly terrorises children who have done things. His tale’s change throughout the regions of the world. There is no specific appearance for the Bogeyman for the simple fact that he is created from someone's thought. WIth that being said, the Bogeyman embodies whatever fear the child has into its appearance. Parents are commonly heard using the Bogeyman as a scare tactic towards children to start behaving well. The adults in the U.S often tell their kids if they keep misbehaving that the Bogeyman will come get them in their sleep. The bogeyman has played a major role in today’s and our past societies by becoming a monstrous character who keeps careful watch over naughty children.
