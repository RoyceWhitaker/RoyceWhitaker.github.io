Dracula


Location: Romania

Time Period: 1879-1898

The monster, Count Dracula, is formerly known as a fictional character in the book Dracula. The character Count Dracula was influenced by a Roman ruler by the name of Vlad Dracula who also went by the name of Vlad the Impaler. He is a human monster who has the attributes of a vampire giving him the ability to heal their wounds and add years to their own lifespan by simply drinking blood . Dracula is undead and looks like any human being except for his pointy ears and dark, moody eyes. He is formerly known as one of the oldest vampires in existence leading folktales of real world landmarks showing Count Dracula’s impact on the world. For example, the Bran Castle is a national monument in Romania which is said to be the castle that Count Dracula lived in. Thousands of people visit it every year with excitement of seeing how he lived. Today with Dracula’s help, vampires have inspired several different movies, books, and even the fashion industry.
